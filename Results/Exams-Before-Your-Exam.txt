        <h2 data-jump-link='on' id="ddb3ef9aab86469fa0122f69c5d0ee9b" data-jump-text="CISSP Computerized Adaptive Test">Familiarize Yourself with CISSP Computerized Adaptive Test</h2>
<p>(ISC)&sup2; uses Computerized Adaptive Testing (CAT) for all English CISSP exams worldwide. Based on the same exam content outline as the linear, fixed-form exam, CISSP CAT is a more precise and efficient evaluation of your competency. Learn what to expect when taking your CISSP CAT exam <a href="/Certifications/CISSP/CISSP-CAT">here</a>.</p>
        <h2 data-jump-link='on' id="2dc8f11cea5e4fea933be36198630662" data-jump-text="Rescheduling or Cancelling an Exam">Rescheduling or Cancelling an Exam</h2>
<p>Once you schedule your exam, you have up to 365 days to reschedule and sit for your exam.</p>
<p>To <a rel="noopener noreferrer" title="Link opens in a new tab" href="https://home.pearsonvue.com/isc2/contact" target="_blank">reschedule or cancel your exam appointment</a>, you must contact Pearson VUE:</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Online</strong> at least 48 hours prior to your exam or</li>
    <li><strong>By phone</strong> at least 24 hours before your exam</li>
</ul>
<p>Pearson VUE charges a reschedule fee of U.S. $50 and a cancellation fee of U.S. $100. If you do not sit for your exam within 365 days of your initial scheduled exam date, your exam fee will NOT be refunded.</p>
        <h2 data-jump-link='on' id="223f66df4afc4463bf0a264c834cb1a9" data-jump-text="Requesting Special Accommodations">Requesting Special Accommodations</h2>
<p>(ISC)&sup2; provides reasonable and appropriate accommodations for its exams with people who have demonstrated a need for test accommodations. If you wish to request an accommodation, complete the <a rel="noopener noreferrer" title="Link opens in a new tab" target="_blank" href="/-/media/Files/Accommodations-Form.ashx?la=en&amp;hash=715BEBD49F7198069B410D62F75ADF24CAE858BD">Accommodation Form</a> and return it to (ISC)&sup2; <strong>prior to registering for your exam</strong> with the following information to <a href="mailto:examadministration@isc2.org">examadministration@isc2.org</a>:</p>
<ul>
    <li>An explanation of the accommodations you need</li>
    <li>Documentation supporting the accommodation</li>
    <li>The exam you want to take</li>
    <li>The exam location</li>
</ul>
<p>Test accommodations are individualized and considered on a case-by-case basis. Once an accommodation is approved by (ISC)&sup2;, we&rsquo;ll send it to Pearson VUE Accommodations. Please allow two to three business days for Pearson VUE to get this information. Then, call Pearson VUE at +1.800.466.0450 or +1.952.905.7025, so you can schedule your exam. Please contact ISC2 first. Do not start by scheduling through Pearson VUE&rsquo;s website or through their main registration phone line.</p>
<p>Please note, that the purpose of test accommodations is to provide examinees with full access to the test. However, they are not a guarantee of improved performance or test completion.</p>
