<h1 class="h1 bar">Annual Maintenance Fees</h1>
<p>Annual Maintenance Fees (AMFs) are used by ISC2; to support the costs of maintaining the ISC2; certifications and related support systems. Certified members and Associates of ISC2; also gain access to a wide array of valuable, rewarding <a href="https://www.isc2.org/Development">professional development</a> opportunities and <a href="https://www.isc2.org/Member-Resources/Member-Benefits">member benefits</a> that deliver a robust return on this annual investment.</p>

<p><strong>Certified Members AMFs</strong></p>

<p>ISC2 certified members pay a single Annual Maintenance Fee (AMF) which is due each year upon the anniversary of their certification date. Members only pay a single AMF regardless of how many certifications they earn. AMFs for members with multiple certifications are due on their earliest certification anniversary.
</p>

<p>For members who hold the following certifications, CGRC, CCSP, CISSP, CSSLP, and SSCP, there is an AMF of U.S. $125. For members who only hold our CC certification, the AMF is U.S $50.</p>


<p><strong>Associates of ISC2; AMFs</strong></p>

<p>Associates of ISC2; pay an AMF of U.S. $50 which is due each year upon the anniversary of achieving their associate status. </p>

<p><a href="https://www.isc2.org/policies-procedures/member-policies">Learn more about our Membership Policies and Procedures here</a>.</p>
