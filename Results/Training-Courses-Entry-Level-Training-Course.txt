<h1 class="h1 bar">Official ISC2 Certified in Cybersecurity℠ Training Course Outline</h1>
    <h2>Introducing your ultimate starting point for an exciting career — Certified in Cybersecurity℠</h2>
<p>Take the first step to a rewarding career and get Certified in Cybersecurity from ISC2, the world’s leading cybersecurity professional organization known for the CISSP®. You don’t need experience — just the passion and drive to enter a field that opens limitless opportunities around the globe.</p>
<p>Features of the course vary by delivery method and include:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Live instruction</li>
    <li>Peer discussions</li>
    <li>Knowledge checks and end-of-chapter quizzes</li>
    <li>Course assessments</li>
    <li>Chapter-by-chapter study sheets with key points, terms and definitions</li>
</ul>

<h3>Who Should Attend</h3>
<p>If you’re looking to join a dynamic and rewarding workforce, get Certified in Cybersecurity and join the ranks. This certification is ideal for those who are:</p>

<ul>
<li>A current IT professional</li>
<li>Looking to transition from another field into cybersecurity</li>
<li>A college student or recent graduate</li>
</ul>

<p>Ideal candidates for Certified in Cybersecurity are:</p>
<ul>
<li>Problem solvers</li>
<li>Creative</li>
<li>Analytical and critical thinkers</li>
<li>Excited by the opportunity to learn</li>
<li>Enjoy both working in a team and independently</li>
</ul>

<h3>Course Agenda</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Chapter 1: Security Principles</li>
    <li>Chapter 2: Business Continuity (BC), Disaster Recovery (DR) & Incident Response Concepts</li>
    <li>Chapter 3: Access Controls Concepts</li>
    <li>Chapter 4: Network Security</li>
    <li>Chapter 5: Security Operations</li>
    <li>Chapter 6: Course Summary and Exam Details</li>
</ul>
<p><em>Note: Course materials are organized by chapter not domain, which may result in domains or individual domain topics being covered in a different order than what appears in the exam outline.  The chapter structure allows us to properly cover the exam domains while supporting a more cohesive learning experience.</em></p>
