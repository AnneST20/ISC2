<h1 class="h1 bar">ISC2 Professional Conduct (Ethics) Committee</h1>
<h2>Committee Charter</h2>
<p>This committee is a standing committee to assist ISC2 in the review of allegations of ethical misconduct of ISC2 members. It is established to work with management and:</p>

<ol>
<li>Assist ISC2 in overseeing the application of the ISC2 Code of Ethics as it relates to Candidate eligibility.</li>
<li>Provide recommendations relating to candidates whose background requires approval prior to sitting for an examination.</li>
<li>Maintain the Candidate Background Eligibility Requirements, and periodically review and recommend revisions, as appropriate, to the Board of Directors.</li>
<li>Deliver recommendations concerning the application and enforcement of the ISC2 Code of Ethics as it relates to the ISC2 members.</li>
<li>Maintain the ISC2 Code of Ethics, and periodically review and recommend revisions, as appropriate, to the Board of Directors.</li>
<li>Offer recommendations relating to ISC2 members who have ethics complaints filed against them.</li>
<li>Exercise such other powers, authority and responsibilities related to ethical misconduct of candidates or members as may be determined.</li>
</ol>

<p>Learn more about the <a href="/Ethics">ISC2 Code of Ethics</a> and our Ethical Complaint Procedure.</p>
