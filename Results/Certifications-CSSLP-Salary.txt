<h1 class="h1 bar">How much does a CSSLP make?</h1><p>A Certified Secure Software Lifecycle Professional (<a href="/-/media/ISC2/Certifications/Exam-Outlines/CSSLP-Exam-Outline-Sept2020.ashx?la=en&amp;hash=C5AD530F3363C64143F4ADE559352EC22CA9F5EC">CSSLP</a>) validates proficiency necessary for authentication, authorization and auditing throughout the software development lifecycle.</p>

<p>The CSSLP certification is one of the top 10 IT certifications that increased in value during 2020 with CSSLP holders earning an increase in 13%, according to <a rel="noopener" title="Link opens in a new tab" target="_blank" href="https://footepartners.com/collections/pay-index">research</a> by Foote Partners.</p>

<p>In addition, ISC2; conducts its own annual <a href="https://www.isc2.org/Research/Workforce-Study">Cybersecurity Workforce Study</a>. This study provides information security professionals insights crucial to their careers and strategies for recruiting them for employers.*</p>
