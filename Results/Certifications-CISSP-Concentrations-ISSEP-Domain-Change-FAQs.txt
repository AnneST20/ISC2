<h1 class="h1 bar">Domain Change FAQs for CISSP-ISSEP</h1>


<div class="faq">
<div class="faq-marker">Q:</div>
<div class="faq-text question">
<p><strong>Why are changes being made to the CISSP-ISSEP exam? </strong></p>
</div>
<div style="clear: both;">&nbsp;</div>
<div class="faq-marker">A:</div>
<div class="faq-text">ISC2; has an obligation to its membership to maintain the relevancy of its credentials. These enhancements are the result of a rigorous, methodical process that ISC2; follows to routinely update its credential exams. This process ensures that the examinations and subsequent continuing professional education requirements encompass the topic areas relevant to the roles and responsibilities of today's practicing cybersecurity engineering professional.</div>
</div>
<div class="faq">
<div class="faq-marker">Q:</div>
<div class="faq-text question">
<p><strong>How is the CISSP-ISSEP exam changing?</strong></p>
</div>
<div style="clear: both;">&nbsp;</div>
<div class="faq-marker">A:</div>
<div class="faq-text">
<p>The content of the CISSP-ISSEP has been refreshed to reflect the most pertinent issues that cybersecurity engineering professionals currently face.</p>
<p>As a result of the content refresh, we have updated some of the domain names to describe the topics accurately. <div class="faq">
<div class="faq-marker">Q:</div>
<div class="faq-text question">
<p><strong>Why do domains for ISC2; credential exams change?</strong></p>
</div>
<div style="clear: both;">&nbsp;</div>
<div class="faq-marker">A:</div>
<div class="faq-text">Domains change because it is a reflection of a change in the knowledge, skills and abilities, as indicated by experts through the Job Task Analysis process.</div>
</div>
<div class="faq">
<div class="faq-marker">Q:</div>
<div class="faq-text question">
<p><strong>When will these changes go into effect?</strong></p>
</div>
<div style="clear: both;">&nbsp;</div>
<div class="faq-marker">A:</div>
<div class="faq-text">The exam changes will go into effect on November 13, 2020.</div>
</div>
<div class="faq">
<div class="faq-marker">Q:</div>
<div class="faq-text question">
<p><strong>In what language will the refreshed CISSP-ISSEP exam be available?</strong></p>
</div>
<div style="clear: both;">&nbsp;</div>
<div class="faq-marker">A:</div>
<div class="faq-text">The refreshed CISSP-ISSEP exam will be available in English only.</div>
</div>
<div class="faq">
<div class="faq-marker">Q:</div>
<div class="faq-text question">
<p><strong>Will this change the number of questions or the time required to take the CISSP-ISSEP exam?</strong></p>
</div>
<div style="clear: both;">&nbsp;</div>
<div class="faq-marker">A:</div>
<div class="faq-text">Yes. The CISSP-ISSEP exam will be reduced to 125 items from 150. ISC2; has decided to reduce the number of exam items from 125 operational and 25 pre-test items to 100 operational and 25 pre-test items. The testing time of three hours remains the same. </div>
</div>
<div class="faq">
<div class="faq-marker">Q:</div>
<div class="faq-text question">
<p><strong>Why is the number of questions being reduced? </strong></p>
</div>
<div style="clear: both;">&nbsp;</div>
<div class="faq-marker">A:</div>
<div class="faq-text">The decision to reduce test length for the CISSP-ISSEP exam was made to maintain consistency across all ISC2; exams </div>
</div>
<div class="faq">
<div class="faq-marker">Q:</div>
<div class="faq-text question">
<p><strong>If I have been studying for the CISSP-ISSEP exam with material that focuses on the current domains, will I be sufficiently prepared to take the new exam without additional study?</strong></p>
</div>
<div style="clear: both;">&nbsp;</div>
<div class="faq-marker">A:</div>
<div class="faq-text">ISC2; exams are experience-based that include experience-based questions that cannot be learned by studying alone. If you already have the experience in the domains covered in CISSP-ISSEP and believe that you have sufficiently studied those domains, you should feel confident that you are qualified to take the new exam and pass it. ISC2; cannot guarantee you will pass the exam.</div>
</div>
<div class="faq">
<div class="faq-marker">Q:</div>
<div class="faq-text question">
<p><strong>Do these updates affect the experience requirement for the CISSP-ISSEP?</strong></p>
</div>
<div style="clear: both;">&nbsp;</div>
<div class="faq-marker">A:</div>
<div class="faq-text">No. For the CISSP-ISSEP, a candidate is required to have a minimum of two years of cumulative work experience in one or more of the five domains of the CISSP-ISSEP CBK.</div>
</div>
