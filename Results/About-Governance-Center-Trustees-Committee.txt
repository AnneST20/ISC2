<h1 class="h1 bar">ISC2 Center for Cyber Safety and Education Board of Trustees</h1>
<h2>Committee Charter</h2>
<p>The Center for Cyber Safety and Education Board of Trustees is established to:</p>

<ol>
<li>Identify, study and offer options to the Board regarding governance, budget, fundraising, and other issues considered critical to the Center for Cyber Safety and Education.</li>
<li>Provide oversight and strategic guidance to the Center for Cyber Safety and Education.</li>
<li>Assist the Center for Cyber Safety and Education staff by setting priorities, leveraging contacts, fundraising, and providing input into crafting appeals and becoming advocates for the Centerâ€™s programs with potential funders and members.</li>
<li>Liaise between the membership organization 501(c)(6) and the charitable separate fund 501(c)(3), to ensure alignment on topics and initiatives of joint interest.</li>
<li>Issue scholarships and grants to individuals to fund their education, research and acquisition of research-related technology in the field of information security or any related field.</li>
<li>Conduct educational programs for staying safe and secure online; conduct research and analysis of the information security systems sector for the benefit of, and broad distribution to, the general public; conduct such other activities and programs in furtherance of charitable, educational or scientific purposes.</li>
</ol>

<p>Learn more about the <a href="https://www.iamcybersafe.org/s/board-of-trustees" target="_blank">Center for Cyber Safety and Education Board of Trustees</a>.</p>
