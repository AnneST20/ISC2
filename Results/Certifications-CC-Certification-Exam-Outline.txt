        <h2 data-jump-link='off' id="6597b6af25bd4697b69a39141286f0a5" data-jump-text="">About Certified in Cybersecurity</h2>
<p>ISC2 developed the Certified in Cybersecurity (CC) credential for newcomers to the field, to recognize the growing trend of people entering the cybersecurity workforce without direct IT experience. Getting Certified in Cybersecurity provides employers with the confidence that you have a solid grasp of the right technical concepts, and a demonstrated aptitude to learn on the job. As an ISC2 certification, those who hold the CC are backed by the world’s largest network of certified cybersecurity professionals helping them continue their professional development and earn new achievements and qualifications throughout their career.</p>
<p>The topics on the CC exam include:</p>

<div class="row ul-cols">
<div class="col-sm-6">
<ul>
<li>Security Principles</li>
<li>Incident Response, Business Continuity (BC) and Disaster Recovery (DR) Concepts</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="col-sm-6">
<ul>
<li>Access Controls Concepts</li>
<li>Network Security</li>
<li>Security Operations</li>
</ul>
</div>
